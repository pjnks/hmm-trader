Critical Finding — Shadow Tracker Architectural Gap

The Shadow Tracker's P&L computation path (live_trading_citrine.py L298–327) writes entry_price = scan.current_price and exit_price = scan.current_price directly, with no fee or slippage adjustment. The live engine by contrast applies _apply_slippage() at L1173 and TAKER_FEE at L1105.

Every shadow A/B comparison to date has therefore carried a ~28 bps phantom tailwind. The Grinold result that appeared to show 5.2× IR superiority for the relaxed cohort was correct in direction but insufficient in magnitude once friction is priced in.
